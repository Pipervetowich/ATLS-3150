Types of Sushi

Traditional Rolls

This types of sushi have the contents wrapped in seaweed and rice.

  • Dragon Roll
  • Caterpillar Roll
  • California Roll

Sashimi

This type of sushi has a bed of rice and the fish laying ontop of the rice.

  1. Sake (Salmon)
  2. Maguro (Tuna)
  3. Unagi (Eel)

Nigiri

This type of sushi is the same as sashimi, but without the rice on. It is just the fish.

  • Yellowtail
  • Ebi (Shrimp)
  • Tako (Octopus)
